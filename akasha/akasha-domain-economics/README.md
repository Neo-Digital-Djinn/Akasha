# akasha-domain-economics

Economic systems grounding pack for the Akasha ecosystem.

## Purpose

Maps market and economic system dynamics into Akasha's structural grammar.

Economic systems frequently exhibit behaviors analogous to complex physical
systems including phase transitions, instabilities, and attractor states.

## Structural focus

- market regime shifts
- speculative bubbles
- crashes and corrections
- equilibrium pricing
- liquidity constraints
- macroeconomic attractors
