# Akasha Comics — Canon Rules

## Core Principle
All characters are based on real-world traits exaggerated into superpowers.

---

## Rule 1: Trait Amplification
Powers must always map directly to a real human behavior.
No exceptions.

---

## Rule 2: Always Active
Characters do not transform.
They are always expressing their power.

"POWERS ACTIVATE" = peak intensity, not a switch.

---

## Rule 3: System Balance
The environment (store) is self-correcting:

- Mavis → increases energy/output
- Kelsey → slows and diffuses
- Amanda → enforces order
- Debbie → stabilizes the system

The system never collapses.

---

## Rule 4: No True Villains (for now)
Conflict comes from:
- timing mismatches
- personality friction
- workload pressure

---

## Rule 5: Tone
"Joyfully overwhelming but functional"

Humor comes from:
- exaggeration
- timing
- contrast

NOT from cruelty.

---

## Rule 6: The Store is a Character
The environment reflects:
- energy levels
- emotional state
- system balance

---

## Rule 7: Respect the Source
Characters are inspired by real people.

They must feel:
- recognizable
- respectful
- human
