# Akasha Axioms (Hardened)

1 — Coherence
All accepted models MUST be internally consistent.

2 — Discoverability
Unknown structure MUST be inferable from known structure.

3 — Alignment
Canonical systems MUST align with Axioms, World, and Registry.

4 — Augmentation
Tools MUST increase capability.

5 — Traceability
All claims MUST include provenance.

6 — Modularity
Components MUST be composable.

7 — Transparency
Processes SHOULD be inspectable.

8 — Iteration
Knowledge flows in loops.

9 — Stewardship
Humans retain authority.

10 — Continuity
State and lineage MUST persist.
