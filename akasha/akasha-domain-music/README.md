# akasha-domain-music

Music grounding pack for the Akasha ecosystem.

## Purpose

Maps musical structure and tonal dynamics into Akasha's structural grammar.

Music systems exhibit clear forms of tension, transition, constraint, equilibrium,
and attractor-like behavior, making them a strong candidate for cross-domain mapping.

## Structural focus

- tonal centers
- modulation
- harmonic tension
- cadence and resolution
- rhythmic and harmonic constraints
- attractor-like pull toward tonic
