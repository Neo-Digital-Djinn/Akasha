# akasha-validation

Validation layer for the Akasha ecosystem.

## Purpose

Akasha-validation exists to test whether structural claims made by the discovery engine are supported by grounded domain mappings.

It is the layer that helps distinguish:

- plausible structural suggestions
- unsupported abstractions
- transferable patterns that hold across domains

## Role in the ecosystem

Akasha-discovery generates structural candidates.

Akasha-domain-packs provide grounded domain mappings.

Akasha-validation evaluates whether proposed structures are coherent, comparable, and useful.

## Planned responsibilities

- validate structural candidates against domain packs
- compare candidate roles across domains
- test whether suggested analogies hold under grounding
- provide confidence signals back to discovery
